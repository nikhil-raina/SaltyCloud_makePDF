I am from Bangalore, India. Well, originally from the North of India, to be specific: Delhi. However, I shifted from Bangalore from 2nd grade onwards and had been there till I had to leave for University at the age of 18 years. While I was in Bangalore 2 schools, Greenwood High (ICSE) and Canadian International School Bangalore (IB).

As far as I remember, I loved to fool around, play around, laugh about with friends and absolutely loved building Lego.

The very concept of building and creating my own “Master Piece”, in a way at least, was far too entertaining for me to let go. Plus, at that age, children basically needed some good way to kill their time. I would usually do it by playing cricket, swimming, badminton, eating or just building things with Lego like: monuments, guns, robots cars, bikes and buildings. As time went by, my mother and her sisters started to realize my passion and enjoyment towards “playing”, that’s what they would always say… such novice individuals, with Lego, I started to get more and more Lego puzzles. Each increasing in complexity. Eventually I was introduced to Mind Storm, which was a GAME CHANGER! Having been able to literally program your robot and make it move or sense or hold objects, was mind blowing to me
